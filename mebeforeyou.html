Me Before You

June 3, 2016 · Romance · Drama · 1h 50m

6.8/10 ★

Cast:

Emilia Clarke, Sam Claflin, Janet McTeer, Charles Dance, Brenda Blethyn, Steve Peacocke, Matthew Lewis, Joanna Lumley, and Susan Hampshire.

Me Before You tells the story of Louisa “Lou” Clark, a cheerful and quirky young woman who takes a job caring for Will Traynor, a wealthy and once-adventurous man left paralyzed after an accident. Initially at odds due to their contrasting personalities, Lou and Will gradually form a deep connection that changes both of their lives. The film explores themes of love, personal choice, resilience, and the courage to face life’s challenges, balancing heartfelt romance with moments of humor and emotional depth.

Review

Me Before You is a romantic drama that delivers both heartfelt emotion and bittersweet storytelling. Emilia Clarke brings charm and warmth to Louisa Clark, while Sam Claflin portrays Will Traynor with a mix of wit, depth, and vulnerability. The film effectively explores complex themes such as love, personal choice, and coping with life-altering circumstances, though some critics noted it leans heavily on melodrama. Its strengths lie in the chemistry between the leads, the emotional resonance of the story, and the balance of humor with poignant moments. Overall, it is a touching, if occasionally predictable, love story that resonates with audiences seeking both romance and emotional depth.
